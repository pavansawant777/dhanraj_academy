Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions students ask most often before enrolling.

We offer comprehensive coaching for UPSC Civil Services, MPSC State Services, SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS), Banking (IBPS PO/Clerk, SBI PO/Clerk), Railway (RRB NTPC, Group D, ALP), Defence (NDA, CDS, AFCAT), and various State Police & Government exams. Our dedicated faculty ensures each course is exam-specific and up to date.

Enrolment is straightforward — visit our office or fill in the contact form above. There is no mandatory entrance test; however, we conduct a free diagnostic assessment to help place you in the right batch level (Foundation, Intermediate, or Advanced) so you get the most out of your preparation.

We run multiple batches throughout the day to suit working professionals, college students, and full-time aspirants: Morning (6:30 AM – 9:00 AM), Regular (10:00 AM – 1:00 PM & 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM), Evening (6:00 PM – 8:30 PM), and Weekend Crash batches (Saturday & Sunday). Online live sessions are also available for all courses.

Students receive printed study modules, topic-wise practice sets, previous year question banks, and access to our online portal with recorded lectures, daily quizzes, mock tests, and performance analytics. Current affairs booklets are updated and distributed every month at no extra cost.

Yes. We offer easy EMI plans (2–6 months, zero interest) for all courses. Additionally, merit-based scholarships of up to 50% are available for students who qualify through our scholarship test conducted every quarter. Students from economically weaker sections may also apply for our special fee-concession programme.

Absolutely. Students may switch to a different batch timing once per month at no charge, subject to seat availability. Course upgrades or lateral transfers (e.g., from SSC to Banking) within the first 30 days of enrolment are also allowed after a counselling session with our academic advisor.
