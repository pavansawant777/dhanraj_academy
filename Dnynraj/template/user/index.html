About Dhanraj

महाराष्ट्रातील विश्वासार्ह स्पर्धा परीक्षा केंद्र

Dhanraj was founded in 2016 with a single mission: to make government jobs accessible to every deserving student in Maharashtra. We specialize in MPSC, Police Bharti, Talathi, Vanrakshak, and Gramsevak preparation.

Our Vision

महाराष्ट्रातील प्रत्येक विद्यार्थ्याला दर्जेदार, परवडणारे शिक्षण देऊन त्यांचे सरकारी नोकरीचे स्वप्न साकार करणे.

2016
Founded Dhanraj with 2 faculty members and 50 students in Pune.
